How to Play

SYTEMS DOWN!

Find the missing pieces of your ship from the town locals

You and your crew are on course for the planet Obiwan when your ship is struck by an asteroid. The ship plummets and lands on an unknown place, with 5 essential pieces of the engine scattered. Explore the town nearby and collect these for your mechanic. Talk with the locals and see if they can help. Ready?
